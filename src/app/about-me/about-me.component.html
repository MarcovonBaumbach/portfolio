About me

Hi, I am a web developer from Dortmund, Germany. My interest into the IT world started when I took a programming class at University and started out by creating my first small personal websites over the years, as well as developing simple games with JavaScript.
When I decided to take my skills to the next level, I joined the Developer Akademie to learn front-end development, building professional business apps and working on projects in small teams.
What helped me a lot to grow as a web developer, is my background as a musician. It takes a lot of creativity, focus and a structured approach to master an instrument, which are skills that have been very useful on my journey.
